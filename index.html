The Methodological Lesson

By failing to control for age brackets, a researcher creates a severe confounding variable effect.

Older demographics naturally use less screen time due to lifecycle factors and simultaneously maintain distinct grade metrics, artificially creating a positive trend line when stacked together.

Always stratify psychological fields!
